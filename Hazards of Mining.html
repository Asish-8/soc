Hazards Of Mining

==>Have you ever wondered about the efforts the miners make in making life comfortable for you? What are the impacts of mining on the health of the miners and the environment? The dust and noxious fumes inhaled by miners make them vulnerable to pulmonary diseases.
==>The risk of collapsing mine roofs, inundation and fires in coalmines are a constant threat to miners.
==>The water sources in the region get contaminated due to mining.
==>Dumping of waste and slurry leads to degradation of land, soil, and increase in stream and river pollution.
