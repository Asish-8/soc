Non-Metallic Minerals

==>Mica is a mineral made up of a series of plates or leaves. These sheets can be so thin that a thousand can be layered into a mica sheet of a few centimeters high.
==>Mica can be clear, black, green, red yellow or brown. Due to its excellent di-electric strength, mica is one of the most indispensable minerals used in electric and electronic industries.
